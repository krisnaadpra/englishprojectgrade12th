English Project (Grade 11th)

PROJECT: Write an essay on the Importance of Following Sunnah in Everyday Life.

Introduction:

  • Introduce yourself and the topic.
  • Briefly explain what Sunnah is and its significance in Islam.

Research:

  • Conduct thorough research using reliable sources like the Qur'an, Hadiths, and scholarly articles.
  • Take notes and organize your findings.

Essay Structure:

a. Introduction:

  • Start with a hook to engage the reader.
  • Provide background information on Sunnah.
  • State your essay on why following Sunnah is important.

b. Body:

  • Paragraph 1: Discuss one key aspect of Sunnah and its impact on daily life.
  • Paragraph 2: Provide another example of how following Sunnah benefits individuals and communities.
  • Paragraph 3: Include real-life examples or anecdotes to illustrate your points.

c. Conclusion:

  • Summarize the main points discussed in the essay.
  • Reinforce the importance of following Sunnah.
  • End with a thought-provoking statement or call to action.

Things to be considered:

1. Writing:

  • Draft your essay following the structure outlined above.
  • Ensure clarity, coherence, and logical flow of ideas.

2. Revision:

  • Review and edit your essay for grammar, spelling, and punctuation errors.
  • Make sure your arguments are well-supported and clearly presented.
